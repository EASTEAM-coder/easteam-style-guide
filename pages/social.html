Social.

IG / TikTok / Pinterest. Captions at 12px, never bigger. In-graphic text small and tracked wide. Lifestyle only on Story (5/7 rule). Same brand, feed-dense scale.

Per platform

Platform 01
Instagram.

Feed is the showcase. Story is candid-only — no catalog stills (5/7 rule). Reels for BTS, fitting, UGC. Pinned grid follows a 3-up rhythm: lifestyle / catalog / dusty-pink graphic.

Feed 4:5 (1080×1350)
Story / Reel 9:16 (1080×1920)
Caption body 12px
Hashtags 5–8, end of caption
Platform 02
TikTok.

Lifestyle, fitting, UGC. Voice-led, not slideshow. Caption sits below the video — keep it under 2 lines, the rest gets cut. Talk-to-her tone, not "babes/girlies".

Video 9:16 (1080×1920)
Caption 12px · 2 lines max
Sound Always-on (trending or native)
Platform 03
Pinterest.

Catalog 3:4 + lifestyle 2:3. Title field works like an eyebrow — Roboto Mono ALL CAPS in the graphic, real product name in the title. SEO-friendly descriptions.

In-graphic text — same three fonts, smaller

Captions on graphics are NOT the place to show off type. Small, tracked wide, off-center. Let the image do the talking. Display moments go to a single word, max.

Caption rhythm — what the text under the post looks like

Daydream's back. Skirt + top. Friday 10AM ET.
Cotton-silk blend, hand-finished hem, four colors. Most wanted, most saved. Set a reminder before it disappears.
#EASTEAM #Daydream #MadeToLoveYou #FoundInBrooklyn #SS26

Line 1 is the headline — the offer or the ask. Same rule as email: meaning in the first sentence.

Line 2–3 fills in the texture. Real words, no filler. Keep total caption under 3 short lines for IG feed — anything more gets truncated in the scroll.

Hashtags last, 5–8 max, mixing brand + collection + discovery. Never first-line hashtag spam.

The 5/7 rule

Of every 7 Instagram Story posts, at least 5 are lifestyle — candid, on-body, in-context. Maximum 2 can be catalog stills or graphics. Story is where she sees the person behind the brand, not the lookbook.

Don't

Don't use Light Pink #F9DDE1 on Warm Ivory in-graphics — invisible. Don't write captions longer than 3 lines. Don't lead with "Hey babes" / "Hi girlies" — talk to her, not at a group. Don't reuse the same caption on three platforms verbatim. Tailor the rhythm to each.
