Section 11

Packaging.

Recyclable, minimal, hand-finished. Sustainability is the baseline — not the marketing angle. Packaging is part of the brand experience, not a logistics afterthought.

The three layers

01

Outer

Recyclable mailer or box. No virgin plastic. Minimal print — wordmark in Staatliches, Pink on Black or Black on Pink only. Address label tucked, not slapped.

02

Inner

Tissue wrap in Warm Ivory with a sticker seal. The wordmark + tagline lockup on the seal. Inside that — the garment, folded the same way every time.

03

The Touch

A thank-you card or hand-signed note. Tier depends on order value. Always something. Always slipped in, never printed onto the box.

Wordmark + tagline lockup

Use the wordmark-plus-tagline lockup (EASTEAM + Made to Love You) on packaging fronts, hangtags, and the press kit cover. Never use the lockup at small sizes — the tagline reads as visual noise under ~10pt.

Materials checklist

  • Mailer: recyclable kraft or compostable poly. No virgin plastic.
  • Box (for sets / high-tier): rigid recycled cardboard, matte finish, single-color print only.
  • Tissue: Warm Ivory, acid-free, FSC-certified.
  • Seal sticker: wordmark + tagline lockup, 1.5" diameter, black-on-pink print.
  • Hangtag: recycled cardstock, lockup printed, knotted with natural twine.
  • Card insert: printed on the same stock as the hangtag.
  • No virgin plastic, no exotic skins, no fur, no foiling, no glitter, no embossed logo blocks.

Don't write about it as a feature

Sustainability is woven into every decision we make. It's the baseline, not the selling point. Don't put "Eco-friendly!" on the box. Don't put "Made sustainably" in every email. The materials speak for themselves.
