Construction · How to build these

AssetArtboardMark sizeTaglineNotes
Solid wordmark 1200 × 600 (2:1) 60% of width — Center. Equal padding top/bottom.
Outline wordmark 1200 × 600 (2:1) 62% of width — Stroke 1.5–2% of cap height. Same baseline, slightly larger compensating for hollow letterforms.
Wordmark + tagline 1200 × 800 (3:2) 55% width 22% width Gap between = 0.5× wordmark cap height. Tagline always solid (never outline).

Clear space

Keep clear space around every lockup equal to the cap height of the wordmark on all four sides. Nothing crosses into it — not photos, not text, not edges.

Don't

Recolor outside the palette. Stretch or skew. Substitute the font. Add a drop shadow, gradient, or stroke (the outline version IS the stroke version — don't add more). Mix outline and solid in the same lockup. Italicize. Rotate. Crop the mark in half "as a design moment." It's a wordmark. Treat it like one.
