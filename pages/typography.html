Section 05

Type.

Three fonts. Each one has one job. Roboto Mono is the default title font across emails, social, and most web sections. Staatliches is the wordmark only — reserved for very rare campaign hero moments.

Surfaces — three scales, three contexts

Surface 01
Web
Body 17–18px · Heads 14–26px
Surface 02
Email
Body 12–13px · Heads 22px Roboto Mono
Surface 03
Social
Body 12px · Heads 11–14px Roboto Mono

Web bodies live at 17–18px because the screen sits two feet from her face. Email and social bodies drop to ~12px because the inbox and feed are dense — bigger looks like shouting. All section titles on email + social use Roboto Mono. Staatliches stays reserved for the wordmark and rare hero moments only.

Font 01 · Body — Jost

She already knows what she wants.

The everyday workhorse. Body copy, captions, button labels, navigation, UI. Sentence case for body, ALL CAPS for buttons and nav.

Weights — when to use which

Light 300
Default body
All running paragraph copy, captions, descriptions, list items. The everyday weight.
Regular 400
Inline emphasis
Names of products / sets, links inside running text, emphasis inside a paragraph.
Medium 500
UI + buttons
Button labels, nav items, form labels, table headers. Anything tappable.

Rules — Jost

Default body is Jost Light 300 — never 400 as the default. Reserve 400 for emphasis. Use 500 only for UI / buttons / table headers. Nothing heavier than 500. Don't italicize Jost — ever.

Font 02 · Headers + Eyebrows — Roboto Mono

Most wanted, most saved.

Every header, every eyebrow, every tag, every table column header. Always ALL CAPS, always tracked wide. Keep it small to mid (10–24px). Goes ugly at display sizes — Staatliches handles those.

Rules — Roboto Mono

Always ALL CAPS. Minimum tracking +0.06em — usually +0.08–0.24em depending on size. Cap at 24px on web, 18px on email, 14px on social. Never set Roboto Mono as a display headline. Staatliches is the display font. Never italic.

Font 03 · Display — Staatliches

EASTEAM

The wordmark — and very little else. Reserved for the EASTEAM logo and rare hero moments (campaign cover, lookbook front, packaging print). Don't use it in emails. Don't use it as a title in social posts. Don't use it for everyday display.

For everyday titles — including the headline inside every email and social graphic — use Roboto Mono at 22px or smaller.

Rules — Staatliches

Rare campaign moments only. The wordmark, and the occasional hero / lookbook cover / packaging print. Never in emails. Never as a section title in social. Never for body. Never for UI. Never below 18px. 0% letter-spacing, no exceptions. Never italic.

Pairing — how the three stack

Every EASTEAM block follows the same rhythm — eyebrow, display, header, body, CTA. Same five layers, every surface. Just different sizes.
