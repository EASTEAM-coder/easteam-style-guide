Section 04

Themes.

Two canvases, two different pinks. Light is the default for the website, every email, and most surfaces. Dark is for the fun moments — campaign covers, packaging fronts, statement drops.

DEFAULT
— Surface 01 · Primary
Light Theme.
Warm Ivory base. Deep Dusty Pink as the only accent that's actually visible against cream. The everyday EASTEAM canvas.
Background#F5F0EC
Primary text#0A0807
Secondary#6B6661
Accent#8E4F58
Pop#C49099
Body type12–18px
Use for easteamny.com (default), every email, decks, lookbooks, line sheets, hangtags. Web body 17–18px; email body 12–13px.
OCCASIONAL
— Surface 02 · For fun
Dark Theme.
Page Black canvas. Light Pink as the accent. Use for moments where the brand wants to be loud — campaign covers, packaging fronts, a single drop landing page.
Background#0A0807
Primary text#F5F0EC
Secondary#B8B2AC
Accent#F9DDE1
Pop#C49099
Body type14–18px
Use for Campaign hero pages, ad cards, packaging exteriors, occasional dark sections inside a light email. Statement moments, not the everyday.

Rule

Don't use Light Pink #F9DDE1 on Warm Ivory — it disappears. Don't use Deep Dusty #8E4F58 on black — it goes muddy. Each pink has its theme. Don't cross them. If a page can't decide, default to Light.
