Sizes — one system, four sizes

Every button type uses the same scale. Padding is intentionally tight on the vertical axis — buttons should feel snug, not balloon-y.

Rules

Never two Primary on the same screen — they compete and conversion drops. Never substitute Ghost for an add-to-cart or checkout. Always preserve the 4px radius on rectangular buttons and 999px on Pills. Always meet 36px min-height on Medium+ for tap targets. Don't bloat vertical padding "for breathing room" — it kills the snug feel.
